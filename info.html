This TV Signal Finder uses your GPS location to caculate the bearing and distance to TV transmitters across the UK.

The top 10 transmitters are identified ranked by either signal strength or shortest distance.

The strongest signal is generally most appropriate, however in occasional blind spots a local lower power transmitter may provide better reception.

Hold your phone flat, face up, choose a transmitter from the list then rotate until the arrow turns green.

The centre of the compass indicates if the signal is horizontally or vertically polarised and the ariel should be adjusted appropriately.
